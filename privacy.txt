<h3>Privacy Policy for AFLPools Membership</h3>

Effective Date: 2023

Welcome to AFLPools! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you become a member of our exclusive club. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Personal Information: When you join AFLPools, we collect the following personal information:

Full name
Contact information (email address, phone number, address)
Date of birth
Gender
Payment information (if applicable)
1.2 Membership Information: We may also collect information related to your membership, including your membership status, preferences, and any communications you have with us.

2. How We Use Your Information
2.1 Membership Services: We use your personal information to provide and enhance the membership services we offer. This includes managing your membership, communicating with you about club events and activities, and personalizing your experience.

2.2 Communications: We may use your contact information to send you important updates, newsletters, and promotional materials. You can opt-out of these communications at any time.

2.3 Payment Processing: If applicable, we use your payment information to process membership fees and transactions securely.

3. Information Sharing
3.1 Third-Party Partners: We may share your information with trusted third-party partners, such as event organizers or service providers, to facilitate club activities and enhance your experience.

3.2 Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

4. Data Security
4.1 Security Measures: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

4.2 Payment Information: If we collect payment information, it is encrypted using industry-standard security protocols.

5. Your Choices
5.1 Updating Information: You have the right to update and correct your personal information. Please contact us if you need assistance.

5.2 Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving promotional communications by following the instructions in our communications or contacting us directly.

6. Children's Privacy
6.1 Age Restriction: Membership is intended for individuals who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18.

7. Changes to This Policy
7.1 Updates: We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Please review this policy periodically for any updates.

8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [contact@email.com].

By becoming a member of AFLPools, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
